Welcome to Inver!

Description:

Inver is a game that allows people meeting for the first time to initiate a conversation and get them to know more about each other.

How to play:

  • There are two players in this game.
  • Player 1 starts by clicking on Player 1 button.
  • A question will be displayed on the screen and player 1 will read it out to ask player2.
  • player 2 repeat the same process as well.
  • players will switch turns to ask questions until they feel confortable with each other.
